MINIONS

Name: Slanderer
Ability: Each night, choose a player: they might register as evil and as a Demon or Minion until dusk.
Notes: A minion that functionally gives a player the Recluse ability for a day.

Name: Debutante
Ability: Your good neighbors might register as evil and as Minions or Demons. You might register as good and as a Townsfolk or Outsider.
Notes: The misregistration half of Spy that makes its good neighbors into Recluses.

Name: Hexen
Ability: Each night, choose a character (different to last night): they are
poisoned until dusk.
Notes: a poisoner that picks characters, not players.

Name: Stitcher
Ability: Once per game at night, choose a dead non-Demon player: they are
resurrected. If they're a Townsfolk, they're poisoned.

Name: Double Agent
Ability: Once per game at night*, you may choose to register as dead. When you
do, you may vote twice per nomination.
Notes: Designed by Ashley
Flavor Text: "Yes, I faked my death. Don't worry, your's will be real."

Name: Rascal
Ability: Each night, choose a player: you learn who they are. You may choose
to poison you both.
Notes: mentally this sits closest to Widow to me among actual characters
