TOWNSFOLK

Name: Prosecutor
Ability: Each night, choose a player: If you are mad they are evil tomorrow,
learn their character that night.
Notes: A Townsfolk that can learn the roles of multiple players at the
cost of needing to act like you think they're evil, whether or not that's
true. There's a ton of balance nobs to turn here; it could be a once per game,
the storyteller could choose who instead of the player, etc.

Name: Prospector
Ability: Each night, choose a number: you learn if the closest minion is less
or more steps away. If exactly that many, this info is arbitrary.
Notes: text is too long, unsure how to shorten

Name: Surveyor
Ability: Each night, you learn how many steps to the nearest living minion. If
there are none, this info is arbitrary.

Name: Proctor
Ability: Each night, you learn how many evil players voted today.
Notes: Cares about votes, can detect if someone has been turned evil if all
vote

Name: Changeling
Ability: You start knowing a not in play Townsfolk. If you would die at night,
you become that character instead.

Name: Ascetic
Ability: Each night, choose a player: when you choose an Outsider, you both
lose your abilities.
